MANAGING TEAM

Taydy Luis Suarez

Taydy serves as the Creative Director for Cuentame. She generates written content, design structure and original artwork for the site. She is currently working on stories for whippersnappers that help us demystify our dreams and gain inspiration from them.

Vaishali is the Head of Content for Cuenteme. She manages the recording and editing for Cuentame 305 and provides original photographs. She is also the main Writer on the Four Spirits of Kab and built the Four Spirits of Kab companion app.

CONTRIBUTING MEMBERS

Zarela Yaluk Mosquera

Zarela is a designer who cares about people, the environment, and how things work. She uses interaction and product design to improve the way people do things and interact with each other. She is the co-creator of the Four Spirits of Kab.
Outside of Cuentame and Kab she has worked for a variety of clients ranging from museums, zoos, national parks, movie production, retail stores, non-profits, independent professionals and educational institutions.

Ricardo Maldonado

Ricardo is a creator. He somehow can envision whole worlds down to their details and still keep that vision clear enough for others to share. As a co-creator of Kab, he uses his ridiculous pile of talent to foster community. Early on, he learned to perceive a person by their character and not by their ethnic background, despite the negative influences from family and associates. He's one of the pioneers who won't leave a trail of negativity behind.
